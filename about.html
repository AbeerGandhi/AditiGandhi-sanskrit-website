About Aditi Gandhi

A Passion for Sanskrit

Aditi Gandhi has been teaching Sanskrit for over a decade. With a Master's degree in Sanskrit Literature, her teaching methodology focuses on making the language accessible, interesting, and easy to learn. She believes in building a strong foundation of grammar and vocabulary, which empowers students to not only score well but also to appreciate the richness of the language.

Our Mission

Our mission is to demystify Sanskrit and foster a genuine love for the language among students. We aim to provide a supportive and engaging learning environment that encourages curiosity and academic excellence. We are committed to helping every student reach their full potential.
